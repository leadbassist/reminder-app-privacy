## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Pursovere Technologies ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the use of **Your Personal Remind Assistant** (the "App"), available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

By downloading, installing, or using this App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, you must not use the App.

---

## 1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, store, process, or share any personal information or data from users of this App.

All information you enter into the App — including reminder titles, notes, dates, times, categories, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to us or any third party.

---

## 2. No Account Required

This App does not require you to create an account, register, or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifier in order to use the App or any of its features.

---

## 3. No Analytics or Tracking

This App does not include any analytics, tracking, or monitoring tools of any kind. We do not use:

- Third-party analytics platforms (such as Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or similar)
- Crash reporting services that transmit data externally
- Advertising networks or attribution tools
- Session recording or heatmap tools
- Any SDK that collects behavioral, usage, or device data

---

## 4. No Advertising

This App does not display advertisements. We do not work with any advertising networks and do not share data with any advertising partners, because we do not collect any data in the first place.

---

## 5. Local Data Storage

All reminder data, settings, and preferences you create within the App are stored locally on your device using standard local storage mechanisms provided by the operating system. This data is:

- Accessible only to you and to the App on your device
- Not backed up to our servers (any cloud backup, such as iCloud or Google Drive, is governed solely by your device settings and the respective platform's privacy policy)
- Deleted when you uninstall the App, unless your device's backup system retains a copy

We have no ability to access, view, retrieve, or recover your local app data at any time.

---

## 6. Export and Import Features

The App provides optional features that allow you to manually export your reminders to a file (JSON or CSV format) and import reminders from such a file.

When you use the export feature:

- A file is created on your device at your explicit request
- Sharing that file is entirely your decision and is performed through your device's built-in sharing and file management tools
- We do not receive, store, or access any exported files

You are solely responsible for how you handle any exported files, including where you store them and with whom you share them.

---

## 7. Notifications

The App uses local device notifications to alert you of your scheduled reminders. These notifications are generated and delivered entirely on your device by the operating system. No notification content is transmitted to our servers or any external service. We have no access to the content, timing, or delivery status of any notification.

## 8. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with or call upon any third-party services, APIs, or servers. The App operates fully offline and does not require an internet connection to function.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any third-party platform, operating system, app store, or cloud service that you may use in connection with your device.

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because we collect no personal information from any user, no special handling for children's data is applicable.
